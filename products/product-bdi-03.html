  1. Home
  2. Battery Discharge Indicator

Description

DEWAG make Battery Discharge Indicator is designed to monitor the health of battery by monitoring its terminal voltage under loaded condition. It operates directly from the battery being monitored and provides visual indication of the battery status by 3 colored LEDs. The unit is configured to operate for 36V or 48V battery. It also provides relay contact output when battery gets discharged, that can be used for any external application such as tripping the load on battery. It is ideal for use in material handling vehicles such as pallet trucks, walkies, stackers and order pickers as well as other battery operated gadgets.

Features

  • Tri colored (Green, Yellow & Red) LEDs to display state of charge with unique symbols
  • The top Red LED illuminates only when the battery charge drops below 80%
  • The middle Yellow LED illuminates when the battery charge drops below 90%
  • The bottom Green LED illuminates when the battery is fully charged
  • Easy to install
  • Relay energizes when battery charge drops below 80%
  • Time delay feature to avoid nuisance tripping during ramp up

Dimensions

  • Front Bezel: 77mm dia X 60mm depth behind the panel
  • Panel Cutout: 70mm dia
  • Mounting: By clamp at the back of the panel

Technical Specifications

  • Supply Voltage:- 36V or 48V DC battery
  • Red LED: Battery Charge below 80%
  • Amber LED: Battery Charge below 90%
  • Green LED: Battery Charge 100%
  • Relay Contacts: 1 changeover, Rated 6Amp. @ 230V AC/ 3Amp. @48V DC
